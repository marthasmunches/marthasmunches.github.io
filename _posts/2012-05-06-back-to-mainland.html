Photo taken on the Armadale to Mallaig ferry, where Dolly, Mr Snappy and Sizzles enjoyed the view. Since the car thermometer has been above 5 most of the day, we're camping again tonight near Arisaig. Keep your fingers crossed it stays above freezing tonight...
